about us

This cozy place was built in 1932 as a church and then later on was an architect’s office, daycare, butcher shop, BBQ restaurant and bakery. The history goes on. The Park’s fell in love with this cute building and decided to give it a new life. With a tremendous love of cookbooks and cooking, we have curated a dining experience that blends authenticity, sophistication, and innovation. Our restaurant is a tribute to the artistry of Italian gastronomy.

At Tavola JP, every dish tells a story. From delicate handmade pasta to expertly seared seafood and premium cuts of meat, our menu is a journey through the flavors of Italy. Each plate is thoughtfully designed to showcase the richness and simplicity that define true Italian cooking, complemented by an extensive selection of fine wines and artisanal cocktails.

Our ambiance reflects the harmony between modern luxury and old-world charm—warm lighting, elegant décor, and a welcoming atmosphere that invites guests to indulge in an unforgettable dining experience. Whether you’re here for an intimate evening, a business gathering, or a special celebration, our team is dedicated to ensuring every detail exceeds your expectations. We invite you to enjoy the beauty of a meal crafted with precision and heart
